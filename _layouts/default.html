About MAHacks

MAHacks is run for high schoolers, by high schoolers. Our goal is to bring together entrepreneurial-minded and tech-savvy students to form truly impactful projects that last beyond our event.

In the past, we've held events at MIT and Canopy Coworking City. We hope you'll join us for MAHacks III!

Introducing Tracks

At MAHacks III, you can submit your project in one of our tracks and win special prizes provided by our sponsors! We want to promote hacks that solve problems and that you'll be excited to continue after MAHacks.

Medical Technology Track

Help solve a problem in the medical industry and you could win the chance to continue your project with Boston Children's Hospital! More information coming soon.

Entrepreneurship Track

Entrepreneurship is all about solving problems in people's lives. Combine your skills with technology and business to identify and meet the world's needs! More information coming soon.
