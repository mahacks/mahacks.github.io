Where technology meets entrepreneurship

We believe in sustainable, long-term projects that solve community problems. We want your hacks to grow beyond our events and create real impact, and we'll help you get there.

About MAHacks

MAHacks is run for high schoolers, by high schoolers. Our goal is to bring together entrepreneurial-minded and tech-savvy students to form projects that last beyond our event. We want to provide new opportunities for all our participants (with some awesome swag on the side!)


In the past, we've held events at MIT, Canopy Coworking City, and Upstatement. We hope you'll join us for MAHacks IV!

Introducing Tracks

At MAHacks III, you could submit your project in one of our tracks and win the opportunity to meet with professionals in the field that will provide mentorship for growing your hacks into real products and ventures. Our tracks address real problems in the community.
